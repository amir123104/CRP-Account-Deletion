Delete Your CRP Account

If you would like to delete your CRP Focus account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Delete via the app

  • Open the CRP Focus app.
  • Go to Settings.
  • Tap "Account".
  • Select "Delete Account".

Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us at:

atronamir5@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your CRP Focus account.

What data is deleted

  • User account information (email)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Associated Firebase user data

Data retention

Some anonymized or legally required data may be retained for a limited period for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes.

⚠️ After deletion, your account cannot be restored.
